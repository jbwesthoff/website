What I Offer

Services

I help manufacturers and machine owners design, build, and maintain reliable control systems.

Design

I design and implement complete control systems — from electrical cabling to operator interfaces with intuitive graphics — tailored to your machine and workflow.

Support

I troubleshoot across mechanics, hydraulics, electronics, and software. Whether it’s a black box failure or a hidden fault, I quickly analyze, pinpoint, and solve to get you running again.

Automation

I integrate ISOBUS and custom machine controls to improve accuracy, productivity, and ease of use, even in challenging agricultural and off-road environments.
